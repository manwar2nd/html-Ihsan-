About Our Mission

Who We Are We are Eritrean Muslims—members of Al Ihsan, a growing non-profit organization with 50+ dedicated members and decades of combined experience in business and charitable work. We've pledged to support Eritreans worldwide—offering help in difficult times and coming together to celebrate joyful occasions like weddings. Our mission is to uplift, connect, and empower our community as one united family. Our community has proudly supported the Daleel Islamic Organization in establishing the first Eritrean mosque in Toronto, Canada. Together, we're building a stronger future—rooted in unity, service, and faith.
